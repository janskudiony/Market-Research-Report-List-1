<p><p>Inductively Coupled Plasma-Mass Spectrometry (ICP-MS) is an advanced analytical technique used for the detection and quantification of trace elements in various samples. It involves the ionization of atoms in a high-temperature plasma and the subsequent analysis of these ions using mass spectrometry, offering exceptional sensitivity and precision. ICP-MS is widely utilized across multiple sectors, including environmental monitoring, food safety, pharmaceuticals, and metallurgy, for its ability to analyze complex matrices effectively.</p><p>The ICP-MS market is anticipated to grow significantly, driven by an increasing demand for accurate elemental analysis and the rise of regulatory standards in various industries.
