<p><strong>The Underfloor Heating for Residential Buildings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydronic Heating</li><li>Electric Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfloor heating for residential buildings primarily consists of two market types: hydronic heating and electric heating. Hydronic heating involves a system of water-filled pipes laid beneath the floor, using a boiler or heat pump to circulate warm water, providing efficient and even heat distribution. Electric heating, on the other hand, utilizes electric cables or mats installed under the floor surface, offering easier installation and faster warm-up times. Both systems enhance comfort while promoting energy efficiency in residential spaces.</p></p>
