<p><strong>The Portable Ophthalmic Ultrasound Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A- Scan</li><li>B- Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymeter</li><li>Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable ophthalmic ultrasound systems market includes various types tailored for different diagnostic needs. A-Scan provides essential axial length measurements for intraocular lens calculations. B-Scan offers cross-sectional imaging of the eye to help identify various conditions. Combined Scan integrates A and B-scan functionalities for comprehensive assessments. Pachymeters measure corneal thickness, essential for glaucoma evaluation. Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM) allows for high-resolution imaging of anterior structures, aiding in the diagnosis of complex ocular diseases and enhancing treatment planning.</p></p>
