<p><p>The plastic alternative packaging market comprises several types. Starch-based plastics are derived from natural starch sources, offering compostability and biodegradability. Cellulose-based plastics utilize plant cellulose, providing an eco-friendly option with good transparency. Polylactic Acid (PLA) is obtained from fermented plant sugars, known for its biocompatibility and versatility in packaging. Polyhydroxyalkanoates (PHA) are biodegradable plastics produced by microbial fermentation of organic materials. Additionally, the "Others" category includes innovative materials and blends, contributing to sustainable packaging solutions.</p></p>
