<p><strong>The Active Optical Connectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simplex Active Optical Connectors</li><li>Duplex Active Optical Connectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active Optical Connectors (AOC) market is segmented into two main types: Simplex and Duplex Active Optical Connectors. Simplex AOCs transmit data in one direction, making them ideal for applications requiring a single-channel connection, such as sensors or cameras. Duplex AOCs, on the other hand, facilitate bidirectional data transmission, allowing for simultaneous send and receive capabilities, which is essential in data centers and telecommunications. Both types leverage optical technology to enhance connectivity over longer distances with reduced latency.</p></p>
