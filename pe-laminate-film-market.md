<p><p>The PE laminate film market is categorized into three main types: duplex, triplex, and others. Duplex films consist of two layers, typically combining polyethylene with another substrate for enhanced strength and moisture barrier properties. Triplex films feature three layers, offering superior durability and protection, making them suitable for food and pharmaceutical packaging. The "other" category includes specialized films with unique properties or coatings to meet specific industry requirements. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to varying customer needs in packaging solutions.</p></p>
