<p><strong>The Polyurethane in Automotive Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-Component Polyurethane</li><li>Two-Component Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the automotive filter market, polyurethane serves as a key material for enhancing filtration efficiency and durability. One-component polyurethane is pre-mixed and ready for use, simplifying application processes in manufacturing filters. It provides good flexibility and adhesion, making it ideal for various filter designs. Conversely, two-component polyurethane involves mixing a resin and a hardener before application, resulting in a stronger bond and greater resistance to environmental factors. This type is preferred for high-performance filters requiring superior stability and longevity.</p></p>
