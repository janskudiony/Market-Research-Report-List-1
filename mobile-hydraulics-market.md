<p><p>The mobile hydraulics market encompasses various components essential for hydraulic systems in vehicles and equipment. Motors convert hydraulic energy into mechanical power, while pumps generate hydraulic flow. Valves control fluid direction and pressure, and cylinders convert hydraulic pressure into linear motion. Filters maintain fluid cleanliness, and accumulators store energy to manage pressure fluctuations. Transmissions enhance power transfer efficiency, while the "others" category includes miscellaneous components like hoses and fittings, vital for the overall functionality of hydraulic systems in mobile applications.</p></p>
