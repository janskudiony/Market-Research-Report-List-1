<p><p>The vacuum thermoformed packaging market includes various materials, each catering to specific needs. HIPS is favored for its durability and ability to resist impact. PVC offers clarity and versatility but has some environmental concerns. PETG is known for its excellent transparency and toughness. RPET, a recycled version of PET, appeals to eco-conscious consumers. PP provides lightweight, moisture-resistant packaging, while HDPE is recognized for its strength and chemical resistance. Each material plays a crucial role in food, medical, and consumer product packaging.</p></p>
