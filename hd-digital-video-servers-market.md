<p><strong>The HD Digital Video Servers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>M-JPEG Technology</li><li>MPEG-4 Technology</li><li>H.264 Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HD Digital Video Servers market encompasses various technologies for video compression and streaming. M-JPEG (Motion JPEG) technology offers simple, frame-by-frame video encoding, ideal for low-latency applications. MPEG-4 technology enhances compression efficiency, providing better quality at lower bitrates, suitable for streaming over limited bandwidth. H.264 technology, known for its superior compression, efficiently delivers high-definition video while maintaining quality, making it popular for Blu-ray discs, streaming services, and broadcasts. Each technology caters to distinct needs in video delivery and storage.</p></p>
