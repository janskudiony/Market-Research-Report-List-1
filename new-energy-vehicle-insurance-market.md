<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) insurance market is divided into two main types: compulsory and optional insurance. Compulsory insurance is legally required and typically includes basic liability coverage to protect against third-party damages. In contrast, optional insurance offers additional coverage that vehicle owners can choose based on their specific needs, such as comprehensive, collision, or theft protection. This structure ensures that all NEVs have a minimum level of protection while allowing owners to customize their insurance according to their preferences.</p></p>
