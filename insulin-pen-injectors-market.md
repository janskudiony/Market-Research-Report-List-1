<p><p>Insulin pen injectors are categorized into two main types: reusable and disposable. Reusable insulin pens allow patients to replace the insulin cartridge, making them cost-effective and environmentally friendly. They typically feature adjustable dose settings and can be used multiple times. In contrast, disposable insulin pens come pre-filled with insulin and are designed for single-use. They are convenient for patients who prefer not to handle refills, ensuring ease of use but generally leading to higher ongoing costs. Both types cater to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
