<p><ul><li>Metal Shell</li><li>Non-metallic Shell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB enclosure market comprises two primary types: metal shell and non-metallic shell enclosures. Metal shell enclosures, often made from aluminum or steel, offer superior durability, electromagnetic interference (EMI) shielding, and heat dissipation, making them ideal for industrial and electronic applications. Non-metallic shell enclosures, typically crafted from plastic or composites, are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and cost-effective, providing flexibility in design and customization. Both types cater to diverse industries, balancing protection, performance, and aesthetic considerations based on specific requirements.</p></p>
