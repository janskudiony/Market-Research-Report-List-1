<p><ul><li>LLDPE</li><li>LDPE</li><li>HDPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyethylene (PE) film market comprises different types: LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene). LLDPE is known for its flexibility and tensile strength, making it ideal for stretch films and bags. LDPE is more transparent and has a softer texture, commonly used in grocery bags and clear packaging. HDPE, being more rigid and resistant, is utilized in robust applications like containers, bottles, and heavy-duty sacks. Each type serves distinct consumer needs within the packaging industry.</p></p>
