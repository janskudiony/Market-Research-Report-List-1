<p><p>The pneumatic filling machine market is categorized into three main types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Automatic machines offer high-speed filling with minimal operator intervention, ideal for large-scale production. Semi-automatic machines require some manual input while still providing improved efficiency and consistency compared to manual options. Manual filling machines rely heavily on operator skill and are suited for small batch production or limited operations, offering flexibility but lower throughput. Each type serves distinct needs based on production scale and automation requirements.</p></p>
