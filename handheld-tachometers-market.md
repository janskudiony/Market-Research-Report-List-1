<p><strong>The Handheld Tachometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Contact</li><li>Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld tachometers market is divided into two main types: non-contact and contact tachometers. Non-contact tachometers use laser or infrared technology to measure rotational speed from a distance, making them ideal for delicate or inaccessible surfaces. In contrast, contact tachometers require physical contact with the rotating object, typically using a probe or wheel to directly measure speed. Both types serve various industries, including automotive, manufacturing, and maintenance, catering to different measurement needs and operational environments.</p></p>
