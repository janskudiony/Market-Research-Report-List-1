<p><p>The Electronic Printed Circuit Board (PCB) market includes various types catering to diverse applications. Rigid 1-2 sided PCBs have one or two layers, suitable for basic electronics. Standard multilayer PCBs feature multiple layers for complex designs. HDI, employing microvias and build-up techniques, allows for dense component placement. IC substrates are specialized for integrated circuits. Flexible circuits provide adaptability in design, while rigid-flex boards combine the properties of rigid and flexible circuitry, offering versatility in compact spaces.</p></p>
