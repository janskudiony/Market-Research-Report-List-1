<p><p>A horizontal centrifugal pump is a mechanical device designed to move fluids by converting rotational energy from a motor into energy in the fluid. This type of pump features a horizontal orientation, making it ideal for various applications in industries such as water supply, wastewater treatment, oil and gas, and chemical processing. The pump operates by utilizing a rotating impeller that imparts velocity to the fluid, which is then converted into pressure as the fluid exits through the pump discharge.</p><p>The Horizontal Centrifugal Pump Market is showing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for efficient fluid management across various sectors.
