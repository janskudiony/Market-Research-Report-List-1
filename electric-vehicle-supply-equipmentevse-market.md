<p><p>Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE) refers to the hardware used to deliver electricity to electric vehicles for charging. The market is primarily categorized into two types: DC Charging and AC Charging. DC Charging systems provide high-speed charging directly to the vehicle's battery, enabling quick refueling, ideal for commercial and highway applications. In contrast, AC Charging offers a slower, more economical option, typically used in residential and public charging stations, utilizing the vehicle's onboard charger for conversion.</p></p>
