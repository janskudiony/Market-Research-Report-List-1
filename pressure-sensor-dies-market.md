<p><p>The pressure sensor dies market is primarily categorized into two types: piezoresistive and capacitive sensors. Piezoresistive sensors utilize a change in electrical resistance to detect pressure variations, making them ideal for applications requiring high accuracy and sensitivity. Capacitive sensors, on the other hand, measure pressure by detecting changes in capacitance caused by pressure-induced deflection of a diaphragm. Both technologies serve diverse industries, including automotive and industrial automation, offering solutions based on performance needs, cost, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
