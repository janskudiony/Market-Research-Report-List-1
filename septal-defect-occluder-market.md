<p><p>The Septal Defect Occluder market is divided into two main types: Atrial Septal Defect (ASD) devices and Ventricular Septal Defect (VSD) devices. ASD devices are designed to close abnormal openings between the heart's upper chambers, preventing blood flow between them. VSD devices, on the other hand, address defects between the heart's lower chambers. Both types aim to restore normal blood circulation, improve heart function, and minimize complications associated with septal defects, offering effective interventional solutions.</p></p>
