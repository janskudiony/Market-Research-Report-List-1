<p><p>The PCB laminate market comprises several key types, each suited for specific applications. Glass fabric laminates, known for their high strength and thermal resistance, are commonly used in critical electronic components. Epoxy resin laminates offer excellent electrical insulation and durability, making them popular in consumer electronics. Kraft paper laminates provide cost-effective solutions for less demanding applications, while phenolic resin laminates excel in high-temperature environments, often used in industrial machinery and automotive sectors due to their robust performance.</p></p>
