<p><p>The medical remote surgery system market encompasses two main types: Master-Slave Control Robots and Autonomous Control Robots. Master-Slave Control Robots rely on a surgeon's manual input, allowing precise manipulation of instruments via a console, enhancing dexterity and control during procedures. In contrast, Autonomous Control Robots utilize advanced algorithms and AI to perform surgical tasks with minimal human intervention, promoting efficiency and accuracy. Both types aim to improve surgical outcomes and expand access to specialized care, catering to various medical applications.</p></p>
