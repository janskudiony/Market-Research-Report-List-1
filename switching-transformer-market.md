<p><p>The switching transformer market encompasses various types designed for different applications. Push/pull transformers utilize a dual winding configuration to efficiently transfer energy, making them suitable for low to medium power applications. Half bridge transformers involve two switches and are effective for medium power supply, offering better efficiency. Full bridge transformers use four switches to provide higher power output and improved voltage regulation, ideal for demanding applications. Each type addresses specific needs in power conversion and regulation across various industries.</p></p>
