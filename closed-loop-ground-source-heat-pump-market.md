<p><p>The Closed-Loop Ground Source Heat Pump market encompasses three primary types: Horizontal, Vertical, and Surface Water systems. Horizontal Ground Source Heat Pumps are installed in expansive, shallow trenches, making them suitable for large properties. Vertical systems involve deep boreholes, ideal for smaller lots with limited space. Surface Water Ground Source Heat Pumps utilize nearby lakes or ponds as heat exchange sources, offering efficient energy transfer. Each type leverages the earth's stable temperatures for heating and cooling, enhancing energy efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
