<p><ul><li>Detached Electronic Ballast</li><li>Built Electronic Ballast</li><li>Integral Electronic Ballast</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adjustable electronic ballast market is segmented into three main types: detached electronic ballast, built electronic ballast, and integral electronic ballast. Detached electronic ballasts are separate units that control multiple lamps, providing flexibility in configurations. Built electronic ballasts are integrated into lighting fixtures, offering a compact solution for commercial applications. Integral electronic ballasts combine the ballast with the lamp in a single unit, enhancing efficiency and simplifying installation. Each type caters to different needs in commercial, industrial, and residential lighting solutions.</p></p>
