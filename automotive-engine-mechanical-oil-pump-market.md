<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Mechanical Oil Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gear Pump</li><li>Rotor Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine mechanical oil pump market primarily includes two types: gear pumps and rotor pumps. Gear pumps utilize interlocking gears to create pressure and flow, making them efficient for high-performance engines. They are known for their reliability and compact design. Rotor pumps, on the other hand, use rotating elements to transfer oil, providing smooth and consistent flow at varying speeds. Both types are crucial for maintaining lubrication and cooling in engines, ensuring optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
