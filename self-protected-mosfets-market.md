<p><strong>The Self Protected MOSFETs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Self-protected MOSFETs are vital components in power management systems, designed to enhance reliability through built-in protection features. The low side devices are positioned between the load and ground, offering efficient control for applications like motor drivers and power converters. In contrast, high side devices connect between the power supply and the load, ensuring precise voltage regulation and protection for devices requiring higher voltage levels. Both types are essential for safeguarding electronic circuits against overcurrent and thermal events.</p></p>
